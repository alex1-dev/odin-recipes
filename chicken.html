Chicken Noodle Soup is a healthy and hearty choice enjoyed by many in the winter months. Whether you have a cold or crave warm broth, this is the recipe for you!

This chicken noodle soup can be on the table in just 40 minutes! This comforting soup will warm you up from the inside out!

How do you make it, you ask? Then look no further. We have the best secret recipe you'll find!

Ingredients

  • Onions and Celery
  • Broth
  • Chicken
  • Noodles
  • Carrots
  • Basil and Oregano

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about five minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve in a bowl with bread or soup crackers
  5. Enjoy!
